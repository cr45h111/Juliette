How watch connection works:
Tap SCAN — Chrome will show a device picker. Select your GloryFit watch from the list.

The app will attempt to read the standard BLE Heart Rate profile (GATT 0x180D). If your watch exposes this, heart rate streams live into Juliette.

⚠ GloryFit watches often use a proprietary BLE protocol — if the connection succeeds but no HR data appears, your watch doesn't expose the standard profile. Use Manual Entry below instead, or try the Google Fit bridge.

Alternative — Google Fit bridge: In GloryFit Pro → Settings → sync to Google Fit. Then open Google Fit on your phone and note your current readings, entering them manually below.
